The Science of Billboards

The Real World
Is Where Brands Win

OOH — Out-Of-Home advertising — is any advertising that reaches people while they are outside their homes. Billboards, street-facing displays, transit panels. It is the most ancient form of brand communication and, according to the most recent independent research, still one of the most effective tools available to marketers today. Unlike digital formats that fight for attention inside a screen, OOH occupies physical space in the real world — unmissable, unblockable, and always on.

Data Source: Dynata & Insights for MOVE — 2022 OOH Effectiveness Study
